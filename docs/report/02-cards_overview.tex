\FloatBarrier\section{Cards Overview}\label{sec:cards-overview}

The cards set is based on the twenty regions of Italy.

Each card is represented by an image of the region, its total score and the category subscores which it is based upon.
The categories are defined as follows.
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textit{Economy} reflects the financial health and industrial output of the region, primarily based on the PIL (\textit{Prodotto Interno Lordo}, or GDP).
    \item \textit{Environment} is a comprehensive metric covering both the natural and cultural assets. It accounts for the historical heritage, as well as the density of monuments and the beauty of the regional scenery.
    \item \textit{Food} represents the richness of the culinary tradition.
    \item \textit{Special} is a wildcard score assigned to highlight unique regional traits and other characteristics not captured by the other categories.
    \item \textit{Total} is a composite metric representing the arithmetic mean of the four subscores listed above.
\end{itemize}
The scores of \textit{Economy}, \textit{Environment} and \textit{Food} were assigned on the basis of real data, meanwhile the \textit{Special} score was assigned according to subjective criteria.
